Privacy Policy

NexaGen Technology Ltd. operates NexaGen, a learning and technology platform for programming, piano practice, dashboards, Q&A support, and digital services.

Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide directly, including your name, email address, phone number, account details, contact messages, support requests, newsletter signups, ratings, subscription choices, and learning progress.

When you use NexaGen, we may also collect technical information such as browser type, device information, pages visited, usage events, approximate location derived from network data, cookies, and similar identifiers.

How We Use Information

  • To provide learning dashboards, programming challenges, piano practice, progress tracking, and account features.
  • To respond to contact requests, support questions, quotes, and service inquiries.
  • To process subscriptions, payments, trials, and product access.
  • To improve site performance, security, content quality, and user experience.
  • To show analytics and advertising where permitted by law and platform policies.

Cookies, Analytics, and Advertising

NexaGen uses cookies and similar technologies to keep the site working, remember preferences, measure traffic, and understand how users interact with the platform. We use Google Analytics to analyze website usage.

We may use Google AdSense or other advertising services. Google and its partners may use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to NexaGen or other websites. Users can manage personalized advertising through Google Ad Settings.

Third-Party Services

We may use trusted service providers for hosting, authentication, database storage, email delivery, payments, analytics, code execution, and advertising. These providers process information only as needed to support NexaGen services.

Payments

Payment processing may be handled by third-party payment providers such as IntaSend or mobile money services. NexaGen does not store full payment card details. Payment information is handled according to the payment provider's policies and security practices.

Data Protection

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect user information. No online service can guarantee complete security, but we work to reduce risk and limit access to information where possible.

Your Choices

You may contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of personal information associated with your account, subject to legal, security, and operational limits. You can also disable cookies in your browser, though some site features may not work correctly.

Children's Privacy

NexaGen is intended for learners, students, educators, and technology users. If a parent or guardian believes a child has submitted personal information without appropriate consent, they should contact us so we can review and remove it where required.
